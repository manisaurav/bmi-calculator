# BMI Calculator: Calculate Your Body Mass Index Online

## What Is a BMI Calculator?

A BMI Calculator is a simple health tool that helps determine whether your weight falls within a healthy range based on your height and weight. BMI stands for Body Mass Index and is one of the most commonly used screening methods for assessing weight status in adults.

Healthcare professionals around the world use BMI as a quick way to identify whether a person may be underweight, at a healthy weight, overweight, or obese.

## Why BMI Matters

Maintaining a healthy weight is important for overall health and wellness. A BMI calculation can help individuals understand whether their current weight may increase their risk of health conditions such as:

* Heart disease
* High blood pressure
* Type 2 diabetes
* Sleep apnea
* Joint problems

Although BMI is not a perfect measurement, it provides a useful starting point for understanding body weight relative to height.

## BMI Categories

BMI results are generally classified as:

* Underweight: Below 18.5
* Normal Weight: 18.5 to 24.9
* Overweight: 25.0 to 29.9
* Obese: 30.0 and above

These ranges are commonly used by health organizations worldwide.

## BMI vs Body Fat Percentage

Many people confuse BMI with body fat percentage.

BMI measures weight relative to height.

Body fat percentage estimates how much of your body consists of fat tissue.

Two individuals may have the same BMI while having very different body compositions.

This is why many fitness professionals recommend using both measurements.
